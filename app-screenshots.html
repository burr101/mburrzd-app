HOW TO CAPTURE SCREENSHOTS

1. Open Chrome → Press F12 (DevTools)
2. Click the device icon (top-left of DevTools)
3. Set dimensions to 414 × 896 and DPR 3
4. Click the ⋮ menu in DevTools → "Capture screenshot"
5. Repeat for each screen below

This produces 1242 × 2688px — exactly what App Store requires.
